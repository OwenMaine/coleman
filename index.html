Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our mattresses and services.

What types of mattresses do you offer?

We offer a variety of mattresses including memory foam, latex, hybrid, and innerspring mattresses to cater to different comfort and support needs.

Do you provide mattresses in custom sizes?

Yes, we offer custom-sized mattresses to meet your specific requirements. Please contact us for more details.

What is the warranty on your mattresses?

Our mattresses come with a comprehensive warranty that covers manufacturing defects. The duration of the warranty varies based on the mattress type. Please refer to the warranty details provided with your purchase.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we provide competitive wholesale pricing for bulk purchases. Please contact our sales team for more information on wholesale options.

How can I place an order?
